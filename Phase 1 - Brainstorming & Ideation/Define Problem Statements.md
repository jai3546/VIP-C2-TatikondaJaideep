## Customer Problem Statement Template

The Customer Problem Statement framework helps identify the real challenges faced by customers and sellers. By understanding their goals, obstacles, and frustrations, the team can design an e-commerce platform that delivers a seamless, secure, and user-friendly shopping experience.

A well-defined problem statement enables the development team to build meaningful features while keeping customer needs at the center of the design process.

**Reference:** https://miro.com/templates/customer-problem-statement/

---

## Problem Statements

| Problem Statement (PS) | I am (Customer) | I'm trying to | But | Because | Which makes me feel |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **PS-1** | An online shopper looking for products across multiple categories | Find quality products and complete my purchase quickly | I often struggle to locate relevant products and compare available options | Many shopping websites have poor search, limited filters, and cluttered product listings | Frustrated and likely to leave the website without making a purchase |
| **PS-2** | A seller managing an online store | Manage products, customer orders, and inventory from one platform | I spend too much time switching between different tools to handle daily operations | Existing solutions are either complicated, expensive, or lack centralized management features | Overwhelmed and less productive in growing my business |
| **PS-3** | A first-time customer shopping online | Register, browse products, add items to my cart, and make secure payments | I worry about complicated checkout steps and payment security | Some platforms provide lengthy registration processes and unclear checkout experiences | Uncertain about completing my order and hesitant to trust the platform |
| **PS-4** | A returning customer | View my previous purchases, monitor delivery status, and manage my orders | I cannot easily access my order history or current shipment updates | Many platforms provide limited order tracking and poor account management features | Disappointed and disconnected from my shopping experience |
